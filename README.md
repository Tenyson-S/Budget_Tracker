# Budget Tracker

A smart Android application designed to help you manage your personal finances effectively using the popular **50/30/20 rule**. Track your income, expenses, and savings with ease while gaining valuable insights into your spending habits.

-   **50/30/20 Budgeting**: Automatically categorizes your spending into Needs, Wants, and Savings to help you stay on track.
-   **Multi-Account Support**: Manage transactions across different accounts (Cash, Bank, Cards, etc.).
-   **Smart Analysis**: Visual breakdown of your expenses with progress bars and dynamic colors indicating budget health.
-   **Recurring Expense Insights**: Automatically detects and highlights recurring bills and subscriptions so you never miss a payment.
-   **Privacy First**: All data is stored locally on your device using Room Database. No internet connection required for core functionality.
-   **Secure Onboarding**: Guided setup to input your income and create initial accounts.
